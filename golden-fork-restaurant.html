Our Story

Fifteen Years of
Culinary Excellence

Golden Fork was born from a simple belief: that a meal shared with loved ones is among life's greatest pleasures. Chef Isabelle Laurent returned from Lyon with a reverence for classical French technique and an obsession with British seasonal produce.

Our dining room in the heart of Mayfair has hosted royalty, artists, and people celebrating life's most treasured moments for fifteen years. Each evening is orchestrated — from the first greeting to the final digestif.

"Cooking is not just nourishment — it is an act of love made visible."
— Chef Isabelle Laurent, Head of Culinary
